The Importance of Feeling OKRs and Goals in Your Heart

October 3, 2024 Â· Growth

Feeling the Goal

It's easy to move through a quarter and realize you no longer feel connected to a goal. When a goal doesn't seem like it's achieving what you want, it's tempting to drop it. But by that point, it's often too late to adopt a new, meaningful goal.

Setting Goals Ahead of Time

You need to set your goals and plans ahead of time to stay on track. It's easy to overlook, but critical to map out personal development and other goals early.

Storytelling as Motivation

For personal development goals especially, being able to tell yourself a strong story matters. Ask yourself:

  • Why does this goal matter to me?
  • How will achieving this positively affect me?

Internalizing these answers can be a huge source of motivation.

Push for Clarity

If you don't fully understand or connect with a goal, don't ignore it. Have conversations with your manager or teammates to gain clarity and buy-in.
